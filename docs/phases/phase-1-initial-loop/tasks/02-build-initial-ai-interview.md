# Build the initial AI interview

Status: planned

Owner: unassigned

Depends on: Define the life-map domain

## Outcome

Create a first-use conversation that explains Mindland in one sentence, immediately begins with a broad invitation, learns what matters to the user, and produces structured proposals for the meaningful specific islands it discovers. Three to five is typical, while fewer are allowed when the conversation supports fewer.

## Done when

The interview opens broadly, asks one question per message, adapts for roughly five to twelve messages, feels conversational, preserves the user’s words, produces validated structured output, avoids inventing filler islands, handles unclear answers safely, and creates no scored activity.
