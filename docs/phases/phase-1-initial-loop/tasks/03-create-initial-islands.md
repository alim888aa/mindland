# Create initial islands

Status: planned

Owner: unassigned

Depends on: Build the initial AI interview

## Outcome

Turn the interview result into the user’s first small, specific islands, place them coherently on the map, and reveal them through a guide that automatically highlights and briefly explains each island. Keep a Skip control and omit a Next button. Allow rename and remove while keeping at least one island. Split broad interests such as Health into the specific islands the user discusses, such as Fitness, Nutrition, and Sleep.

## Done when

The first islands begin with no completed-activity nodes, persist, render at their small starting stage, retain their purpose and source context, can be renamed or removed during the guided reveal, and can be opened from the map. The reveal does not allow adding an island or removing the final island. Afterward, the map opens for exploration before the user chooses Check in. V1 shows no broad umbrella island above them.
