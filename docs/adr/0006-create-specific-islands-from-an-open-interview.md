# ADR 0006: Create specific islands from an open interview

Status: accepted

Date: 2026-07-17

## Context

The first interview must discover what genuinely matters to the user without forcing a fixed life-category template. Daily activities also need specific destinations such as Fitness, Nutrition, and Sleep.

## Decision

Begin onboarding with a broad invitation and continue adaptively for roughly five to twelve messages. Create the meaningful specific islands supported by the conversation. Three to five is typical, while fewer are valid. V1 does not display broad umbrella islands such as Health.

## Reason

This keeps onboarding conversational, avoids filler islands, and prepares specific islands to become grouped archipelagos in a later version.

## Consequences

The AI output must explain why each island was inferred, separate broad themes from specific islands, and tolerate a variable island count. The reveal flow must lay out and edit that variable set.
